In VOIDFACE, you face off in a challenging bullet-hell arena shooter to try and clear bite-sized boss rush challenges. Learn to dodge their attacks, cast spells to gain an upper hand, and kill everything that gets in your way.

- 27 unique bosses, across 9 stages
- Tutorial and finale stage with special bosses
- Easy and hard mode (even easy mode is quite challenging!)
- One hit and you're dead (two hits on easy mode)
- Various defensive and offensive spells
- Stylized, smooth-rendered pixel graphics
- An original banger soundtrack with dynamic layering for each stage
- Unlockable hats and artworks
